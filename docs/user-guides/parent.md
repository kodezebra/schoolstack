## Troubleshooting

### Can't see my child

- You may have multiple children registered
- Use dropdown or tabs to switch
- Contact school if issue persists

### Grades not showing

- May be start of term
- Grades may not be posted yet
- Check with teacher

### Forgot password

1. Click **Forgot Password** on login
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password

### Payment issues

- Contact school directly
- Use alternative payment method
- Keep receipt as proof

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How often are grades updated?

Grades are posted by teachers after assessments. Typically updated within 1-2 weeks after exams.

### Can I print my child's report?

Yes, use the **Print** or **Download PDF** option in the grades section.

### How do I schedule a meeting with my child's teacher?

Contact the school office to schedule parent-teacher meetings. Check announcements for scheduled meeting days.

### What if I notice incorrect information?

Contact the school administration to report and correct any errors in student records.
